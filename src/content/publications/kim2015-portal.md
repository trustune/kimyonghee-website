## Research Overview

Efficiency analysis of portal service providers using stochastic frontier analysis (SFA) methodology.

## Methodology

### Stochastic Frontier Analysis
- Production frontier estimation
- Efficiency measurement
- Technical inefficiency analysis

### Tobit Regression
- Second-stage analysis
- Determinants of efficiency
- Factor identification

## Key Findings

### Efficiency Determinants
- **Market Share**: Positive impact on efficiency
- **Fixed Assets**: Optimal scale importance
- **Network Effects**: Strong influence
- **Content Quality**: Critical factor

### Industry Characteristics
- Winner-take-most dynamics
- Scale economies
- First-mover advantages
- User lock-in effects

## Implications

- Strategic insights for portal operators
- Market structure understanding
- Investment guidance
- Competitive strategy development
