## Research Overview

Comprehensive efficiency analysis of 96 mobile operators across 40 OECD countries using Data Envelopment Analysis.

## Methodology

- **Sample**: 96 mobile operators in OECD countries
- **Method**: Data Envelopment Analysis (DEA)
- **Data**: Financial and operational metrics

## Key Findings

### Efficiency Patterns
- Significant variation across operators
- Market structure influences efficiency
- Scale effects in mobile operations

### Regional Differences
- European operators: High technical efficiency
- Asian operators: Scale efficiency advantages
- American operators: Mixed performance

## Implications

Strategic insights for:
- Mobile operator management
- Regulatory policy
- Market structure analysis
- Investment decisions
