## Executive Summary

This study analyzes 10-year structural changes in Korea's broadcasting industry revenue (2015-2024).

Broadcasting advertising declined 34.52%, while IPTV grew 99.1%. Government support stands at only 10.15%, the lowest among OECD countries.

## Key Findings

- Total net inflow revenue: 8.89 trillion won (2024)
- Data verification accuracy: 99.50%
- Complete paradigm shift from advertising to subscription model

## Research Methodology

Based on 3,126 official records with cross-validation achieving 99.50% accuracy.

**Researcher**: Yonghee Kim, Ph.D., Sunmoon University
